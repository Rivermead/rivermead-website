About Us

Thirty-Seven Years of
Financial Expertise

The Rivermead Partnership has a history of assisting legal practices and financial companies with expert reports and regulatory advice. Our particular expertise is the diagnosis of financial mis-selling claims — providing certainty to meritorious cases and stopping unwarranted claims from progressing.

Simon Dorrell, Sole Director, brings over 37 years in financial services. He was one of the first to identify the mis-selling of SIPPs by brokers and was instrumental in bringing SIPP Companies' Duties into the regulatory and legal framework. He has succeeded in changing policy at the Financial Services Compensation Scheme with regard to quantum of loss and dual claims for SIPPs.
